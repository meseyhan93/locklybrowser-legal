Lockly — Privacy-First

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Lockly handles your information when you use the app.

Last updated: February 8, 2026

1. About Lockly

Lockly is a privacy-focused browser and secure vault app. It is designed so that your private content stays on your device and under your control.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Lockly is built with a strong privacy-first approach. The app does not collect, store, or send the following data to our servers:

  • Browsing history or visited websites
  • Vault content, files, media, or downloaded items
  • Passwords, unlock codes, or Face ID / Touch ID data
  • Personal identifiers such as name, email, or phone number

All private content is stored locally on your device and is not uploaded to any external server operated by us.

3. Local Data on Your Device

Lockly may store the following types of data only on your device:

  • Encrypted vault items (files, media, archives)
  • App preferences and settings (e.g., ad blocking, privacy options)
  • Subscription status flags used to enable Pro features

This data is managed by the app and your operating system. If you delete the app, this locally stored data may be removed by the system.

4. Subscriptions and Payments

Paid features in Lockly are offered through auto-renewable subscriptions. All payments are processed securely by Apple via the App Store. We do not have access to your full payment information, such as credit card numbers.

You can manage or cancel your subscriptions at any time in your Apple ID account settings.

5. Third-Party Services

Lockly may integrate privacy-respecting third-party services (for example, subscription management or basic analytics) solely for operating and improving the app. These services do not have access to:

  • Your private vault content
  • Your downloaded files or media
  • Your detailed browsing history inside Lockly

Any third-party service that we use is required to handle data in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

6. Data Security

Lockly relies on security mechanisms provided by the operating system, such as on-device encryption and biometric authentication (Face ID / Touch ID, where available). We recommend that you protect your device with a strong passcode and keep your operating system up to date.

7. Children’s Privacy

Lockly is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can remove it.

8. Your Rights and Choices

Because Lockly does not collect or store personal information on its own servers, there is typically no account data for us to modify or delete on your behalf. You can, however:

  • Delete vault content and downloads directly within the app
  • Delete the app to remove locally stored app data from your device
  • Manage your subscription in your Apple ID account settings

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app or in legal requirements. When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Your continued use of Lockly after changes are posted means you accept those changes.
